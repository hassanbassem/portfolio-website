Work Flow

Discover

This is where I get to know you and your business. Together we define your goals, target audience, key challenges, and the purpose of your website. This step ensures we’re aligned from the start and sets a clear direction for the project.

Research

I analyze your industry, competitors, and user needs to identify opportunities that will influence the site structure and user journey. This phase shapes a strategy and a roadmap that guides the design and development.

Design

Using the research, I create wireframes and then translate them into polished visual mockups. Here is where branding (fonts, colors, imagery, style) comes to life, ensuring the site is both user-friendly and visually aligned with your brand identity.

Develop

I turn the designs into a fully functional website. This includes coding, CMS setup, responsive layouts for all devices, animations, and SEO best practices. Everything is tested thoroughly to make sure your site works seamlessly across browsers and devices.

Launch

Once everything is approved and ready, your site goes live! I handle deployment, connect domains, and make sure performance and security are in place. After launch, I can also provide training and ongoing support to keep your site growing with your business.
